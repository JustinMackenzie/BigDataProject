ABOUT SERVICES

SERVICE1

This service will have a descriptive behavior. Data will be queried for either the stock value or the average stock value for the given day, time or year, corresponding to the company the client is interested in.

SERVICE2

The service will allow clients query the CAD-USD exchange rate of any time and the average exchange rate of a day, a month and a year through fixer.io Api.



SERVICE3

This service will allow clients query historical exchange rate.

SERVICE4

This service will combine S1 and S2. The customers would be able to retrieve the stock value of a certain day, or the average of a certain month/year. The clients will be returned with the (already) exchanged value for the amount they had originally queried for.
